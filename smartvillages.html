Faq on smart village

Q1.What are smart villages ?

A1. Smart Village is a concept adopted by national, state and local governments of India, as an initiative focused on holistic rural development, derived from Mahatma Gandhi's vision of Adarsh Gram and Swaraj.

Q2.Why do we need smart villages?

A2.A Smart Village would typically pay attention to e-literacy skills, access to e-health and other basic services, innovative solutions for environmental concerns, circular economy application to agricultural waste, promotion of local products supported by technology and ICT, implementing and taking full benefit of smart technology.

Q3.What makes a village a Smart village?

A3. Abstract Smart Village refers to a concept developed in rural area that provides solutions to problems occurred and improves the quality of life. The main problems faced by rural areas are cover poverty, low level of education, and limited access to technology.

Q4.How can we improve our village ?

A4. Though each village will have its own individual topical solution, the following points are essential: 1.Identify people's needs and priorities. 2.Define activities that can mobilize the complete community. 3.Use resources from running government schemes. 4.Repair and renovate existing infrastructure. 5.Strengthen the Gram Panchayat.

Q5.Is there any smart village in India ?

A5. Chhotkei village in Angul district of Odisha has emerged as the first smart micro grid implementing village in India. The village gets a supply of 30 kWp (kilo watt, peak) Solar-power.
